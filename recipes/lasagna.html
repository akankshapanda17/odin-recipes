Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef and onion in a skillet until browned.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Unroll the lasagna noodles and place them in a 9x13 inch pan.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the pan.
  7. Layer the lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover with the remaining meat sauce and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 5 minutes before serving.

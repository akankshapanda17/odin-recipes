Chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a classic and delicious dessert made with a moist and creamy chocolate frosting. It is a popular treat that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, and mix well.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and mix well.
  5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt; mix well.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Spread the batter evenly into a 9x13 inch pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  9. Let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
